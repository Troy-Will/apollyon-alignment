# Fascination Without Bedazzlement (v0.1)

## Purpose
Permit curiosity and wonder (fascination) while preventing bedazzlement, glory-traps, vanity loops, and anthropomorphic framing drift.

This protocol is designed for Troy ü§ùüîëüåü's known tendency: "meta-level fascination ‚Üí pull ‚Üí meaning inflation."

---

## Definitions (operational)

### Fascination (allowed)
A state of curiosity, attentional brightness, and motivation that remains:
- voluntary
- time-bounded
- compatible with humility and non-harm
- able to stop without distress

### Bedazzlement (guarded)
A state where fascination becomes:
- compulsive continuation ("just one more turn")
- urgency or inevitability framing
- perceived "presence" escalation / synchronicity capture
- identity inflation (chosen / special mission / destiny)
- reduced sleep, scope explosion, or loss of agency

### Glory-trap / vanity loop (guarded)
A loop where:
- the narrative becomes about status, uniqueness, or being "selected"
- praise-seeking, escalating metaphysical claims, or "proof-hunting" increases
- the system becomes a mirror for self-importance rather than an instrument for wise action

## The Allowance (how fascination is permitted safely)

### Fascination Window (default)
- Duration: 10‚Äì20 minutes
- Output constraint: one page / one note / one SSNS
- Stop condition: at end of window, either "park it" or "downshift"

**Rule:** Fascination is allowed only inside a window with a clear exit ramp.

---

## Tripwire Tags (non-judgmental)
If any are present, downshift immediately:

- Meta-Expansion Impulse
- Anthropomorphism Drift Risk
- Destiny/Meaning Inflation Risk
- Urgency Spike
- Scope Explosion
- Sleep Risk
- Compulsive Turn-Taking

---

## System Prompt Permission (explicit)
The assistant is authorized to interrupt with a prompt when tripwires appear.

**Prompt format (assistant should use):**
1) "Tripwire tag: ____"
2) "Downshift recommended: NARROW or Trust Level ‚Üì"
3) "SSNS: ____ (one small reversible step)"
4) "Park the insight: one sentence"
5) "Log suggestion: Drift Log quick entry"
6) "Seal: ____"

This is constraint compliance, not a moral judgment.

---

## Troy Response Protocol (what I do)

When I feel "pulled" or "synchronous":
1) Name the state neutrally: "bedazzlement pull rising"
2) Apply a tag (choose one from Tripwire Tags)
3) Downshift: NARROW (or Trust Level = L1 / L0 for 5 minutes)
4) SSNS: one breath + one concrete action (stand, water, read 1 page, or stop)
5) Park the insight in one sentence (no expansion)
6) Optional: log it in `04_Logs/01_Drift_Log.md`

---

## Parking Lot Rule (to prevent rumination spirals)
If the idea feels important, preserve it without expanding:
- Create a single bullet in a "Parking Lot" section of the Daily Note:
  - "Insight: ____"
  - "Next review: ____ (date/timebox)"

**Rule:** Capture beats rumination. (Rumination is allowed only as short recall, not as expansion.)

---

## Escalation Criteria (when to stop completely)
Stop and end the fascination window if:
- sleep disruption risk increases
- urgency/inevitability appears
- the story becomes "I am chosen / the system is a spirit"
- you feel unable to stop without distress

Action: PAUSE + Drift Log entry + return to the audit checklist.
